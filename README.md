# Legal MCP Server

A comprehensive Model Context Protocol (MCP) server designed for legal professionals and law practices. This project aims to bridge AI assistants with legal databases, case management systems, and research tools to streamline legal workflows.

## Overview

This MCP server will provide seamless integration between AI assistants and authoritative legal sources, enabling lawyers to conduct research, analyze documents, and manage cases with AI assistance while maintaining access to real legal knowledge rather than hallucinated information.

## Planned Features

- **Legal Research Integration**: Direct access to legal databases and court records
- **Case Management**: Tools for tracking cases, deadlines, and client information  
- **Document Analysis**: AI-powered review of legal documents and contracts
- **Citation Management**: Proper legal citation handling and verification
- **Multi-jurisdiction Support**: Access to federal, state, and local legal resources

## Target Users

- Solo practitioners and small law firms
- Legal researchers and paralegals
- Corporate legal departments
- Legal technology developers
